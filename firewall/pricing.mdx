The Crypto Debt Firewall is available on three tiers. The free tier is fully functional with no credit card required and no time limit. It is designed for open source projects, individual contributors, and teams evaluating the platform before committing. Pro unlocks unlimited AI audits and integrations for teams shipping to production. Enterprise adds the compliance and access control features required by regulated industries.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| Semgrep scanning | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| AI audits | 50 per month | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Languages | 5 | 5 | 5 |
| Platforms | GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket | All | All |
| CBOM sync | No | Yes | Yes |
| Policy enforcement | No | Yes | Yes |
| Team notifications | No | Yes | Yes |
| SSO / SAML | No | No | Yes |
| Audit log | No | No | Yes |
| Dedicated support | No | No | Yes |

## Tiers

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Free" icon="gift">
    Includes unlimited Semgrep scanning and 50 AI audits per month. No credit card required. Covers all three supported platforms (GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket) and all five supported languages.

    Best for: open source projects, evaluation, and small teams with low PR volume.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pro" icon="rocket">
    Removes the AI audit limit and adds CBOM sync, policy enforcement, and team notifications. Pro connects firewall findings directly to your Crypto Bill of Materials so every flagged pattern is tracked as a cryptographic asset.

    Best for: product teams shipping to production who need full coverage and workflow integration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise" icon="building">
    Adds SSO / SAML, a full audit log, and dedicated support. The audit log captures every scan, every finding, and every developer feedback signal with timestamps and repository identifiers, supporting SOC 2, FedRAMP, and PCI DSS audit requirements.

    Best for: regulated industries, large engineering organizations, and teams with compliance obligations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Start with the free tier. Upgrade when you need unlimited AI audits or CBOM integration. Semgrep scanning is unlimited on every tier, so the free tier provides real value from day one even before you hit the AI audit limit.
</Tip>
