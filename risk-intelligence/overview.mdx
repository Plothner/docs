## What Crypto Risk Intelligence does

Post-quantum migration starts with knowing what you have. Crypto Risk Intelligence gives you a complete, continuously updated inventory of every cryptographic asset in your environment, scored for quantum risk and mapped against the compliance frameworks that matter to your organization.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Asset Discovery" icon="radar" href="/risk-intelligence/discovery">
    API-based scanning across 40 connectors. Finds TLS certificates, cloud encryption keys, SSH keys, and cryptographic code calls without touching production workloads.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CBOM" icon="table" href="/risk-intelligence/cbom">
    A Cryptographic Bill of Materials for your entire organization. Every asset catalogued with algorithm, key size, expiry, and ownership metadata.
  </Card>
  <Card title="HNDL Risk Scoring" icon="gauge" href="/risk-intelligence/hndl-scoring">
    Harvest Now, Decrypt Later scores combine data sensitivity, quantum timeline, exposure surface, and regulatory pressure into a single 0-100 risk number per asset.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance Engine" icon="shield-check" href="/risk-intelligence/compliance">
    Gap analysis against 13 frameworks including CNSA 2.0, NIS2, PCI DSS 4.0, and GDPR. Deadline tracking built in.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Board Reports" icon="file-chart-line" href="/risk-intelligence/reports">
    PDF reports rendered in seconds via Typst. Portfolio summaries, per-asset migration plans, and executive HNDL briefings.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it fits together

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your infrastructure">
    Point Quanterios at your cloud accounts and on-premises systems using read-only credentials. Discovery runs immediately and on a configurable schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your CBOM">
    Every discovered asset lands in your Cryptographic Bill of Materials. Filter by algorithm, key size, expiry date, or risk score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand your HNDL exposure">
    The scoring engine evaluates each asset across four risk dimensions and generates an AI-written briefing explaining the risk in plain language.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map against compliance frameworks">
    The Compliance Engine checks each asset against the frameworks you care about and highlights gaps with deadline context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export and act">
    Generate board-ready PDFs or trigger migration workflows directly from the dashboard or the API.
  </Step>
</Steps>
